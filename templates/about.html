About Converter by Hakez Labs

Privacy-first, ad-free file conversion.

Our Core Mission: Privacy & Simplicity

Converter is built on the principle that essential utilities should be **simple and private**. We offer a fast, reliable, and completely **ad-free** service for converting files. Unlike many online tools, we enforce a strict policy of **zero data retention**.

Any file you upload, along with the converted output, is **automatically and permanently deleted** from our servers immediately after the download link is provided. Your data privacy is our guarantee.

Technical Foundation (How It Works)

The application is powered by **Django**, providing a secure and scalable backend written in Python. Key architectural choices include:

  • **Template Inheritance:** The layout is centralized in **base.html**, which all other pages extend for consistency.
  • **PRG Pattern:** We use the **Post/Redirect/Get** pattern to separate file submission from display logic, ensuring robust handling of large file uploads.

Current & Future Capabilities

Currently, the service focuses on reliable conversion to and from popular **document formats** (e.g., PDF, DOCX, etc.).

A Note on Documents:

Please be aware that converting complex formats like old **.doc** or heavily formatted **.docx** may result in minor layout discrepancies due to compatibility limits of server-side conversion libraries. We recommend checking the converted output.

Future plans include expanding support to more specialized formats, including archives, audio, and video conversion.
